# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

People who run aio-proxy and manage their own local model gateway. They configure upstream providers, inspect runtime status, and diagnose routing, authorization, or provider problems when requests fail.

## Product Purpose

Provide aio-proxy with one visual control surface for managing providers and model routes without manually editing configuration, while using usage data and request logs for fast troubleshooting. The design succeeds when users can configure the proxy faster and identify failures sooner.

## Positioning

Manage model routing across providers and protocols through one local control plane.

## Brand Personality

Restrained, reliable, and clear. The interface takes cues from Cloudflare's mature operational density, status communication, and configuration flows, keeping the tool professional without adding visual noise.

## Anti-references

Avoid flashy SaaS marketing-site styling: no decorative gradients, glass effects, marketing copy, or motion unrelated to the task.

## Design Principles

- **Operations first:** Every page first helps users configure, inspect status, or diagnose a problem.
- **State must be actionable:** Errors, authorization, health, and routing outcomes must be visible and point to the next step.
- **Unify complexity:** Present different providers and protocols through consistent concepts and interaction language.
- **Dense, not crowded:** Preserve the information density operations require while controlling cognitive load through hierarchy and progressive disclosure.
- **Restraint builds trust:** Visual emphasis serves actions, state, and feedback—not decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Guarantee baseline usability: complete keyboard operation, clearly visible focus states, and readable text and control contrast. No specific WCAG conformance level is currently promised.
